Frequent Questions About Mental Health

What is mental health?
Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act as we cope with life. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices.
What are common mental health disorders?
Common mental health disorders include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, eating disorders, and addictive behaviors.
How can therapy help with mental health issues?
Therapy can provide a safe space to explore the root causes of your mental health issues, offer strategies for dealing with stress and emotions, and introduce coping mechanisms to handle day-to-day challenges.
Can mental health issues be prevented?
While not all mental health issues can be prevented, some practices can reduce the risk and severity. These include maintaining a healthy lifestyle, staying connected with others, getting regular exercise, and seeking professional help when needed.
